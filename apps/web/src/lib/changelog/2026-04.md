#### Add dynamic lighting to your scenes

You can now place dynamic lights on your maps to create atmosphere and highlight important areas. Lights can be customized with different colors, sizes, and styles including candle flicker and pulse effects. Perfect for torches, magical glows, or dramatic spotlights. [#428](https://github.com/Siege-Perilous/tableslayer/pull/428)

#### Fixes

- Fixed an issue where selecting an effect (fire, ice, etc.) for a drawing layer would not save properly, causing the effect to revert to a plain color on reload. [#428](https://github.com/Siege-Perilous/tableslayer/pull/428)
- Fixed an issue where the playfield cursor would stop updating from the editor after periods of inactivity or when switching to a scene in a different game session. [#424](https://github.com/Siege-Perilous/tableslayer/pull/424)
