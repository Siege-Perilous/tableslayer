#### Smoother performance on older devices

Table Slayer now adapts its rendering quality to your hardware. A new performance setting in the play controls defaults to Auto, which watches your frame rate and automatically lowers quality — resolution, fog detail, weather density, and drawing effects — when a device can't keep up. You can also pin the quality to High, Medium, or Low manually. [#463](https://github.com/Siege-Perilous/tableslayer/pull/463)
